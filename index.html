About Me

Hello! I am currently working on the Typhoon project, supported by SCB 10X, a venture capital and innovation firm, with a focus on advancing language technology for Thai.

I completed my PhD in NLP from the University of Cambridge, supervised by Prof. Mark Gales, with a research focus on summarization and NLP evaluation. I publish mostly in NLP conferences (ACL, EMNLP, *ACL) and speech conferences (Interspeech, ICASSP).

Previously, I worked as an applied scientist intern at Amazon. I hold a B.A. and an M.Eng. in Information and Computer Engineering from the University of Cambridge, obtained in 2019.
