About Me

Hello! I am a 2nd-year PhD student in the Machine Intelligence Lab in the Engineering Department, the University of Cambridge.

I am working under the supervision of Prof. Mark Gales, and my PhD is fully funded by Cambridge Trust (Cambridge International & St John's College Scholarship).

Before starting my PhD, I was an undergradute student in Engineering at the University of Cambridge (2015-19). I am a member of St John's College (matriculated in 2015).

Research Interests

  • Automatic Summarisation and Summary Assessment
  • Text Generation
  • Natural Language Processing
  • AI for Language Learning (e.g. Grammatical Error Correction/Detection)
  • Speech Processing
  • Deep Learning & Machine Learning
