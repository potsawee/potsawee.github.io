About Me

Hello! I am a PhD student in NLP @ the Machine Intelligence Lab in the Engineering Department, the University of Cambridge. My PhD is under the supervision of Prof. Mark Gales, and I am funded by the Cambridge International & St John's College Scholarship.

Currently, I also work on Typhoon, an open-source Thai large language model.

Previously, I worked as an applied scientist intern at Amazon. Before PhD, I obtained B.A. and M.Eng. degrees in Information and Computer Engineering from Cambridge in 2019.

Research Interests

  • Summarization and Text Generation
  • Text Evaluation & LLM Hallucination
  • Question Generation and Answering
  • Low-Resource NLP
